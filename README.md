# JKEIT.IN - Online Learning Management System

## Overview

JKEIT.IN is a complete PHP and MySQL based Online Learning Management System (LMS) designed to provide digital education services through a centralized platform. The system allows students to purchase courses, access learning resources, attend recorded classes, take online examinations, and generate certificates and marksheets after successful course completion.

The platform also includes a Franchise Management System that enables franchise partners to register, purchase franchise access, and manage their own students through a dedicated dashboard.

The system consists of three major modules: Admin Panel, Student Panel, and Franchise Panel.

---

## Student Workflow

Students must first register and purchase a course through the integrated payment gateway.

After successful payment, students receive access to their personal dashboard.

### Student Features

* Student Registration & Login
* Course Purchase System
* Payment Gateway Integration
* Student Dashboard Access After Payment
* Automatic Student ID Card Generation
* Course Syllabus Access
* Notification Management
* PDF Notes Download
* Recorded Video Classes
* Learning Progress Tracking
* Online Examination System
* Result Management
* Certificate Generation
* Marksheet Generation
* Profile Management

---

## Franchise Workflow

Franchise partners must register and complete payment verification before receiving dashboard access.

### Franchise Features

* Franchise Registration
* Franchise Payment System
* Franchise Dashboard Access
* Student Registration Management
* Student Monitoring
* Enrollment Tracking
* Performance Tracking
* Franchise Reporting

Franchise users can add and manage only their own students through their dedicated dashboard.

---

## Admin Panel Features

The Admin Panel provides complete control over the entire platform.

### Admin Capabilities

* Website Content Management

* Student Management

* PDF Notes Upload
* Recorded Video Class Upload

* Question & Answer Management

* Login Monitoring
* Enrollment Tracking

* Marksheet Management

Admin can view:

---


## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to create a complete digital learning ecosystem where students, franchise partners, and administrators can efficiently manage educational activities through a secure and user-friendly platform.
